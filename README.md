# Facebook-ads-perfomance-analysis-and-visualization
A comprehensive analysis of marketing data to evaluate advertising profitability. The project focuses on identifying patterns in spending and Return on Marketing Investment (ROMI), as well as discovering correlations between various ad metrics.
Tasks: data analysis and processing – data visualization by constructing graphs and charts, which depict the total amount of campaign advertising costs and ROMI in each of the campaigns.
Project Results: developed an analytical tool that enables tracking the dynamics of ad spend and ROMI over time, comparing the effectiveness of different advertising campaigns and identifying anomalies and the distribution of profitability metrics.
